Special Comments
SC1) Please note, the numberOfShares and pricePerShare are multipled together to determine the amount of the transaction.  This means if a gift is added with an amount of 5, a numberOfShares of 2, and a pricePerShare of 3, the amount of the gift in eTapestry will be 6.
